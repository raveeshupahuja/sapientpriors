About SapientPriors

SapientPriors is an AI research company dedicated to achieving human-level intelligence through fundamental advances in machine learning and reasoning.

Our work focuses on developing models that can understand, learn, and reason about complex problems with human-like capability.

Research Areas

Reasoning & Inference

Developing advanced reasoning capabilities through recursive decomposition and reinforcement learning.

Self-Aware Systems

Building models with calibrated confidence and uncertainty quantification.

Meta-Learning

Enabling models to learn how to learn, adapting quickly to new tasks and domains.

Efficient Learning

Cost-effective training methods using LoRA and curriculum learning approaches.
