Research

Exploring the foundations of continually learning autonomous agents

Continually Learning Agents

Our primary research focus is building agents that can learn and improve continuously throughout their lifetime.

The Challenge

Traditional AI systems are static after training. They cannot adapt to new situations, learn from mistakes, or improve based on experience. We're working to change that by developing agents that:

  • Learn from every interaction and experience
  • Adapt their behavior based on feedback and outcomes
  • Transfer knowledge across different tasks and domains
  • Improve their performance over extended periods of operation
  • Operate autonomously with minimal human intervention

Our Approach

We're combining multiple research directions to achieve continual learning:

  • Online Learning: Updating models in real-time from streaming data
  • Meta-Learning: Teaching agents how to learn efficiently
  • Memory Systems: Developing architectures that maintain and utilize long-term knowledge
  • Personalization: Adapting to individual users and contexts
  • Robust Reasoning: Building reliable decision-making under uncertainty

Research Areas

Meta-Learning

Enabling models to learn how to learn, adapting quickly to new tasks and domains. This is fundamental to building agents that can continuously acquire new skills and knowledge efficiently.

Reasoning & Inference

Developing advanced reasoning capabilities through reinforcement learning. Agents need robust reasoning to make decisions and learn from complex environments.

Self-Aware Systems

Building models with calibrated confidence and uncertainty quantification. Continual learning requires knowing what you know and what you need to learn.

Efficient Learning

Developing scalable and cost-effective training methods for advanced AI systems. Continual learning must be practical and sustainable for real-world deployment.

Personalization

Creating systems that adapt to individual users and contexts. Personalization is a key capability for agents that learn continuously in diverse environments.

Memory & Knowledge Retention

Developing architectures that can maintain long-term knowledge while continuing to learn new information without catastrophic forgetting.
